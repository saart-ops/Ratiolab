# Topic: DOJ vs. Cal-Maine Foods, Versova, and Hickman's Egg Ranch

The DOJ and 17 state attorneys general filed a civil antitrust lawsuit against three of the nation's largest egg producers in 2026.
The three companies named in the lawsuit are Cal-Maine Foods, Versova, and Hickman's Egg Ranch.
The producers allegedly coordinated their bidding on the Egg Clearinghouse Inc. platform to artificially inflate prices.
The manipulation targeted Urner Barry, a market reporting company whose benchmark prices are used in contracts throughout the egg industry.
